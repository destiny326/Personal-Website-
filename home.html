Welcome

Welcome to Dessy's Space. This is my Nightmare Journal — a dark corner of the internet for anyone who loves horror, mystery, and the art of fear. Here, we dig into films, stories, and strange true events that make your skin crawl. It's not about jump scares — it's about what stays with you after the lights go out.

What You'll Find Here

  • Unfiltered horror reviews — films, books, and series that actually get under your skin.
  • Breakdowns of dark folklore, myths, and the real stories behind horror legends.
  • Curated lists for every mood — from slow-burn dread to full-blown nightmare fuel.

Why I Started This Blog

I've always been fascinated by what scares us — the tension, the silence, the things we can't explain. Dessy's Space is where I explore that obsession — peeling back the layers of fear, one story at a time.

Follow along or drop me a message if you've got a horror worth watching.
